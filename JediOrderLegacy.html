The Jedi Order

High Ranks

Grand Master

Position: Leader of the Order.

Responsibilities: Makes final decisions, ensures balance and unity.

How to obtain: Elected by JOL or chosen by the owner. Only 1 member can have this rank at a time.

Master of the Order

Position: Second-in-command, right hand of the Grand Master.

Responsibilities: Assists in leading JOL, oversees the High Council, and maintains order.

How to obtain: Chosen by the Grand Master from the most trusted JHC members. Only 1 member can have this rank at a time.

Jedi High Council

Position: Elite advisors and councilors of JOL.

Responsibilities: Hosts events, manages training programs, and co-leads the Order.

How to obtain: Handpicked by the Grand Master from the most loyal and skilled members.

Ambassador

Position: Chief Diplomatic Envoy of the Jedi Order.

Responsibilities: Serves as primary liaison between JOL and external groups, negotiates agreements, resolves conflicts diplomatically, and protects the Orderâ€™s interests.

How to obtain: Personally selected by the Grand Master based on exceptional diplomatic skill, loyalty, and strategic thinking. Only 1 member can have this rank at a time.

Chief of Security

Position: Leader of the Temple Guards and Honour Guards.

Responsibilities: Oversees security and defense, leads Temple and Honor Guards, enforces protocols during crises, defends sacred locations, and enforces the Jedi Code.

How to obtain: Selected by the Grand Master based on loyalty and leadership. Only 1 member can have this rank at a time.

Battle Master

Position: Highest ranking Instructor of the Order.

Responsibilities: Oversees all combat training, specializes in lightsaber techniques and battlefield strategy. Leads Lightsaber Instructors and Exotic Weapons Specialists.

How to obtain: Granted only to loyal members with significant contributions. Only 3 members can have this rank at a time.

Exotic Weapons Specialist

Position: Expert in non-traditional weaponry

Responsibilities: Master various exotic weapons, train others, develop new combat techniques, and preserve ancient weapons knowledge.

How to obtain: Approved by the Battle Master based on skill and contributions.

Jedi High Council+

Position: Subcouncil for specialized governance

Responsibilities: Assist the main council, oversee specialized projects and maintain Jedi wisdom.

How to obtain: Selected by Grand Master or Master of the Order based on organizational skills and passion for knowledge.

Senator

Position: Representative of the Jedi Order

Responsibilities: Diplomatic relations, peace negotiations, maintaining Order's neutrality and alliances.

How to obtain: Selected by JHC+ or Ambassador for exceptional communication and negotiation skills.

Artisan

Position: Artistic contributor to the Order

Responsibilities: Create artwork and designs reflecting Jedi values and culture.

How to obtain: Selected by Grandmaster or MOTO based on artistic merit.
